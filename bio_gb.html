Bio

My name is Andrea Fiori, 1988, and I want to be a web developer. The world of the Internet has always interested me since I was a child and later being able to create sites from scratch fascinated me more and more. For me, being able to combine a very rigid code with a design that has no limits, if not the imagination of those who create it, is a wonderful combination. I have never studied to make websites, but thanks to start2impact university I have learned the languages ​​of html, css and I am continuing to study to learn other languages such as Javascript to improve my technique. In addition to the Web, I am also interested in photography (especially nature photography - www.fotografixnatura.it) and videomaking. Working as a full stack developer is my ambition and thanks to commitment and perseverance I am sure I can reach my goal.
